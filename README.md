## Features
- Secret hidden answers in the petition (type your hidden answer surrounded by periods like `.red.`).  
- Interactive card flip animation.  
- Clean and mysterious UI.

## How to Use
1. Type your petition in the petition box.  
   - To include a hidden answer, type a period `.`, then your answer, then another period `.`  
2. Type your question in the question box.  
3. Click **Reveal the Cards** to see the cards flip and reveal the hidden answer.
